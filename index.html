who is
pony?

My name is Rébecca Chemouil, I was born in 1979 and I founded Pony in 2019. When I was little, I dreamed of saving gorillas and being a French teacher.
I took a slight detour, finally spending twenty years in marketing and communications, in agencies and with advertisers.
It was out of a deep desire to move the boundaries and assume that she wasn't like anyone that Pony was born.
Far from marketing clichés and dogmas, I adopt a unique, joyful and authentic style in order to support clients from all backgrounds and meet wonderful people so that I never get bored.
I like to be alongside those who have the courage to create, to build, to invent, to help them reveal themselves.
I listen, analyze and identify the smallest detail, even the most buried, to make each subject a unique case in order to build together a positioning, an identity, a storytelling and a strategy that only resembles you .
